Who Am I?

I am a Software Engineering student with a passion for learning and developing my personal and professional skills.

I got started in tech at the age of 10, when during a class of mine I decided to learn how to code in python. I used it to help me with homework, but I quickly began learning system administration, webstack development, application programming, and eventually full stack software development.

Currently, I am focusing on full-stack web applications at Neumont University, but in my free time I like to play video games, go camping, or attend various events such as the Renaissance Festival (pictured to the right).
